INFORMATION ABOUT THE GAME, PART 1

 

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Together with 2 other randomly selected participants, you form a triad that stays together during the experiment. You and two other participants file a joint tax report for a (fictitious) company. Additional to your reward, you will receive a bonus conditional on the company's after-tax profit. 

Our fictitious company had 800 pence as actual corporate income in the last fiscal period. The task is to decide how many pence will be reported as the income of the company to the fiscal authority. While doing so, consider that 25 percent of the reported income will go to the tax payment. Since you belong to a triad, you will have the opportunity to communicate about the proposal with the other members in a chat box for a maximum of 5 minutes.

Note: While reading the information pages, you are asked to answer some questions about the game. Answering these questions is only a check of your understanding and is not payout relevant.
